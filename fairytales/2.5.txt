The cat's mouth soon again began to water for the delicious goods.

"All good things come in threes,"
    he said to the mouse.
"I have been asked to be godfather again.
"The child is totally black, only it has white paws.
"Otherwise it has not a single white hair on its whole body.
"This only happens once every few years. You will let me go, won't you?"

"Top-Off. Half-Gone,"
    answered the mouse.
"They are such odd names, that they make me stop and think."

"Here you sit at home,"
    said the cat,
"with your dark gray fur coat and long braid of hair capturing fantasies.
"That is because you do not go out in the daytime."
